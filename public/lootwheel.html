Lootwheel

Spin the wheel to win NFTs, game boosts, or additional SPINZ tokens using your earned WHEEL tokens.

SPIN

Spin to Win

The lootwheel is your gateway to exciting rewards in the MINIGAME ecosystem. Spend your hard-earned WHEEL tokens for a chance to win valuable NFTs, SPINZ tokens, and game boosts.

Each spin costs 100 WHEEL tokens and gives you a chance to win one of the following rewards:

  • Rare NFTs with special in-game abilities
  • SPINZ tokens to increase your reward multiplier
  • Game boosts for temporary advantages
  • Exclusive character skins and cosmetics

The more SPINZ tokens you hold, the better your chances of winning rare rewards!

Possible Rewards

NFT

NFT Rewards

Win rare meme-character NFTs that unlock special abilities in games, such as double speed, extra points, or immunity to attacks.

SPINZ

SPINZ Tokens

Win SPINZ tokens to increase your reward multiplier and earn more WHEEL tokens while playing games.

BOOST

Game Boosts

Win temporary boosts that give you advantages in games, such as increased size, faster movement, or bonus points.

How It Works

1

Play Games to Earn WHEEL

Play any of our 3-minute games to earn WHEEL tokens. The amount you earn depends on your performance and your SPINZ token multiplier.

2

Connect Your Wallet

Connect your Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom) to the MINIGAME platform to access the lootwheel and spend your WHEEL tokens.

3

Spin the Wheel

Spend 100 WHEEL tokens to spin the lootwheel and get a chance to win valuable rewards.

4

Claim Your Reward

Your reward will be automatically sent to your connected wallet. NFTs and boosts will be available for use immediately in games.
