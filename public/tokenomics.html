Tokenomics

Explore the economic system powering the MINIGAME ecosystem with our dual-token model.

Our Tokens

SPINZ

SpinZ Token

The main trading token of the MINIGAME ecosystem with a total supply of 1 billion. SPINZ is a fully tradable token that powers the entire platform.

Holding SPINZ tokens increases your reward multiplier, allowing you to earn more WHEEL tokens while playing games.

SPINZ can be acquired through trading on PumpSwap, winning on the lootwheel, or participating in community events.

WHEEL

WheelZ Token

The in-game reward token earned through gameplay. WHEEL is primarily used as in-game currency within the MINIGAME ecosystem.

Earn WHEEL tokens by playing our games, with rewards based on your performance and SPINZ holdings.

Spend your WHEEL tokens on the lootwheel for a chance to win NFTs, game boosts, or additional SPINZ tokens.
