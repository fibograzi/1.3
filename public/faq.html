Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MINIGAME, our tokens, games, and more.

What is MINIGAME?

MINIGAME is a Web3 gaming platform featuring fast-paced 3-minute games on the Solana blockchain. Our platform combines fun, accessible gameplay with blockchain technology, allowing players to earn rewards in the form of WHEEL tokens, which can be used to acquire NFTs and other in-game benefits.

How do I get started with MINIGAME?

To get started with MINIGAME:

  1. Create a Solana wallet (like Phantom)
  2. Connect your wallet to our platform
  3. Choose a game to play
  4. Start earning WHEEL tokens based on your performance
  5. Use your WHEEL tokens on the lootwheel to win NFTs and other rewards

What games are available on MINIGAME?

MINIGAME currently offers three games:

  • Neon Agar: A 3D Agar.io variant in cyberpunk style
  • Cyber Slither: A Slither-like game with neon aesthetics
  • Grid Conquest: A Paper.io-like territory control game

All games are designed to be played in short 3-minute sessions and feature our signature neon-cyberpunk 3D style.

How do I earn rewards while playing?

After each game session, you'll earn WHEEL tokens based on your performance. The amount of WHEEL tokens you earn is also affected by your SPINZ token holdings, which provide a multiplier effect. The more SPINZ you hold, the higher your reward multiplier will be.

What are SPINZ and WHEEL tokens?

MINIGAME uses a dual-token system:

  • SPINZ: The main trading token with a total supply of 1 billion. Holding SPINZ increases your reward multiplier when earning WHEEL tokens.
  • WHEEL: The in-game reward token earned through gameplay. WHEEL can be spent on the lootwheel for a chance to win NFTs, game boosts, or additional SPINZ tokens.

How does the token multiplier system work?

The token multiplier system increases your WHEEL token earnings based on how many SPINZ tokens you hold:

SPINZ in Wallet Multiplier
0 – 99,999 0.1×
100,000 1×
100,001 – 499,999 2×
500,000 – 1,499,999 3×
1,500,000 – 4,999,999 4×
5,000,000 – 24,999,999 5×
≥ 25,000,000 8×

What are MINIGAME NFTs?

MINIGAME NFTs are unique digital assets on the Solana blockchain that provide special abilities and bonuses in our games. Each NFT represents a meme-character with specific in-game benefits, such as double speed, increased token rewards, or immunity to attacks.

How do I get NFTs?

There are two main ways to acquire NFTs on MINIGAME:

  1. Lootwheel: Spend your WHEEL tokens to spin the lootwheel for a chance to win NFTs
  2. Direct Minting: Use WHEEL tokens to mint NFTs directly from our marketplace

Once acquired, NFTs will automatically be available for use in games, providing their unique benefits.

Which wallets are supported?

MINIGAME supports all major Solana wallets, including:

  • Phantom
  • Solflare
  • Backpack
  • Sollet
  • Slope

We recommend using Phantom wallet for the best experience.

How do I connect my wallet?

To connect your wallet to MINIGAME:

  1. Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top right corner of the website
  2. Select your wallet provider from the list
  3. Approve the connection request in your wallet
  4. Once connected, you'll see your wallet address and token balances in the top right corner
