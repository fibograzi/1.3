Our Vision

At MINIGAME, we're revolutionizing the gaming industry by creating fast-paced, accessible games that seamlessly integrate with blockchain technology. Our vision is to build a thriving ecosystem where players can enjoy quick gaming sessions while earning real value through our dual-token economy.

We believe that gaming should be fun, rewarding, and accessible to everyone. That's why we've created a platform of 3-minute games that anyone can pick up and play, while still offering the depth and rewards that keep players coming back for more.

Our neon-cyberpunk aesthetic isn't just a visual choice—it represents our forward-thinking approach to gaming and blockchain integration. We're building the future of gaming, one 3-minute session at a time.

Our Roadmap

Q2 2025

Platform Launch

Official launch of the MINIGAME platform with three initial games: Neon Agar, Cyber Slither, and Grid Conquest. Introduction of SPINZ and WHEEL tokens on Pump.fun.

Q3 2025

NFT Collection Release

Launch of our first NFT collection featuring meme-characters with special in-game abilities. Implementation of the lootwheel system for NFT distribution.

Q4 2025

Mobile App Release

Launch of MINIGAME mobile application for iOS and Android, allowing players to enjoy our games and earn rewards on the go.

Q1 2026

New Games & Tournaments

Addition of three new games to the platform and introduction of competitive tournaments with SPINZ token prizes.

Q2 2026

Governance & DAO
